Antitrust, State aid & competition

Competition both at the European and the national level

The Firm provides qualified assistance in the interpretation and application of European Union antitrust law (cartels and anti-competitive practices, abuse of dominant position) and State aid.

The Firm has substantial experience in this area in proceedings before the courts of the European Union.

Types of problems

  • anti-competition agreements
  • abuse of dominant position
  • concentrations
  • State aid
  • unfair competition

Cases

Beispiele der Beschwerden die Kanzlei eingerichtet hat:
