This table only lists those cases that have reached the Tribunal or the Court of Justice. The great majority of cases take place before national Courts, other European institutions, or follow administrative proceedings.

Nota Bene

For reasons of confidentiality (in particular, to avoid identification of the client), for each case the roll number was omitted. For each case are reported:

  • the letter T to indicate proceedings before the Tribunal (until November 30, 2009 called Court of First Instance);
  • the letter C (or with no letter) to indicate proceedings before the Court of Justice;
  • the letter P to indicate appeal proceedings before the Court of Justice against decisions of the Court;
  • the year of rooting of the case;
  • the subject matter and any clarifications.
